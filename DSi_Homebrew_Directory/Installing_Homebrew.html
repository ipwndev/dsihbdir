Entry Vectors

 

DSiHax:  Most “commonly” used was written by Yellow8.  Commonly referred to as SudokuHax, is arguably one of the easiest entry points to the DSi’s 166MHz ARM9.  It was updated on August 25th in 2011 to support a total of four games.  When the hack launches it attempts to load boot.nds on the SD card. The compatible games are Sudoku, Guitar Rock Tour, FieldRunners, and Legends of Exidia.  There are a few downsides to this entry method: 

    -your gamesave will be deleted

    -DSi needs to have one of the four games installed and be on a FW less than 1.4.2

    -Install relies on Hackmii’s servers.
